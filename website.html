Empower Your Choices:

From Allergens to Ingredients

Create an Account

The Problem:

While we all need food to live, some foods can do more harm than good if you ingest it. The problem is sometimes the food label is not explicit about certain hidden allergens or other harmful ingredients.

The Essential Questions:

So how do you know if something is safe to eat? Especially if you have allergies?

What if I am prescribed a medication and have to be careful of my diet?

Our Solution:

Create a tool that would help users determine whether the food is safe by either:

  1. identifying possible allergens in the food
  2. list the negative drug interactions between food and certain prescription medications
